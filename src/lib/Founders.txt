Rodrigo Fernandes is a co-founder of Augusta Labs since September 2023. Before joining João Cerejeira to establish Augusta Labs, Rodrigo worked at Sword Health, a Portuguese startup, as an associate to the CEO. He left the academic world to pursue his goals in the realm of entrepreneurship where he has successfully proved his value.


João Cerejeira has been a co-founder of Augusta Labs since September 2023. Before teaming up with Rodrigo Fernandes to found Augusta Labs, João worked at McKinsey & Company, an American consultancy, as a Business Analyst. Academically, he holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics and Management. He also made a huge impact in FEP Junior consulting while serving as President of the general assembly.
